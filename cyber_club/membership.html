How to Join the Club?

Membership is open to UAUT students currently will enrolled in any degree program. You can get added to the Cyber Security Club email list by emailing our secretary........, at uautcyberclub@domain.edu, and then keep up to date with all of the clubs current and future activities. There is no attendance requirement or monetary fee to participate in the club. We encourage all members to come as often as their schedules allow. We hope to see you at our next meeting!
